## Time

```text
1 ns = 10^-9 seconds
1 us = 10^-6 seconds = 1,000 ns
1 ms = 10^-3 seconds = 1,000 us = 1,000,000 ns
1s   = 10^1 seconds  = 1,000 ms = 1,00,000 us  = 1,000,000,000 ns
1d   = 84,600s 
```
